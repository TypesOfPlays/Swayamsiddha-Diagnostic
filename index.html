What services does Swayamsiddha Diagnostic offer?

We offer advanced DR (Digital Radiography) X-ray imaging, complete blood count (CBC), blood sugar, lipid profile, thyroid function test, liver & kidney function tests, urine analysis, and more.

What is DR (Digital Radiography) X-Ray?

Digital Radiography (DR) is a modern X-ray method that captures the image digitally instead of using film. It is quicker, cleaner, and helps doctors view the image clearly.

When can I collect my reports?

Most routine reports are made available as soon as they are completed. The exact collection time depends on the type of test. Our staff will inform you when your report is ready for collection.
